Our Story

Out story is so difficult to describe in a few words. We believe the supermoon on 14th Nov has done something special to us. Brought two people together who were meant to be but dumb enough not to tell each other & staying at 8155 km away that time.

  • First We Meet

    June, 2015

    A very brief, abrupt and completely unplanned meeting! Met her outside her university, to gift her with a pair of shoes which I thought would suit her perfectly. Landed in front of each other, the upfront “Hii” with a little bit of shyness. As some more time passed by, things became a little “less shy” and then we talked, made fun of things around us and shared our first ice cream together. Indeed, a memorable first meetup.

  • First Date

    December 28, 2015

    First date was the first day I saw him after I came back. At the mall. In front of the cash counter, haha! I looked at him and he was smiling. All of it made me feel that the person whom I’ve been seeing virtually through the screen for all this time is right in front of me! It was a strange yet a very soul-satisfying feeling. Truly felt I was back home.
